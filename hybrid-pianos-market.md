<p><p>The hybrid piano market includes two main types: acoustic hybrids and digital hybrids. Acoustic hybrids combine traditional piano components with digital technology, offering an authentic playing experience along with features like silent modes and sound recording. Digital hybrids, on the other hand, are fully electronic instruments that mimic acoustic sound and feel, often featuring advanced connectivity options, built-in accompaniments, and a range of sound customization. Both types cater to diverse player preferences, blending classical and modern musical needs.</p></p>
